## 📱 POSTING SCHEDULE

### Instagram
- Frequency: 3-4x per week
- Best times: Tue-Thu, 6-9 AM
- Content: Tips, Reels, Stories
- Hashtags: 30 per post

### TikTok
- Frequency: 3-5x per week
- Best times: 6-10 AM, 7-11 PM
- Length: 30-60 seconds
- Hook: First 3 seconds critical

### Pinterest
- Frequency: 5-10 pins per week
- Best times: 8-11 AM, 7-11 PM
- Format: Vertical (1000x1500px)
- CTR: Link to your site

### Reddit
- Frequency: 1-2x per week
- Subreddits: r/gardening, r/vegetablegardening
- Style: Helpful, not promotional
- Tone: Genuine, authentic

### Email
- Frequency: 1-2x per week
- Best day: Tuesday-Thursday
- Best time: 9 AM
- Content: Tips + 1-2 products
