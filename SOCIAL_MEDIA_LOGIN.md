## Getting Started

### Pinterest Setup (High Priority - Drives Most Traffic)
1. Log in to https://pinterest.com/login
2. Go to **Business Account** settings
3. Set up **Rich Pins** for your blog posts (drives more clicks)
4. Create **Pinterest Boards** for:
   - Garden Tools & Supplies
   - Vegetables to Grow
   - Garden Design Ideas
   - Seasonal Planting
   - Pest Management

5. **Create Pins** from your blog articles:
   - Use 1000x1500px dimensions
   - Add keywords in description
   - Link directly to your articles
   - Post 5-10 pins per week

### Instagram Setup
1. Log in to https://instagram.com/accounts/login
2. Go to **Settings > Account > Switch to Professional Account**
3. Set up **Instagram Business Profile**
4. Add link to your website in bio
5. Create content calendar:
   - 3-4 posts per week
   - Use Reels for 5x more reach
   - Post behind-the-scenes content
   - Share gardening tips
