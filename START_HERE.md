## 🚀 Launch Your Site (1 Hour)

### Step 1: Affiliate Setup (15 minutes)
```
1. Go to: amazon.com/associates
2. Sign up with your Amazon account
3. Get your Tracking ID (looks like: yourname-20)
4. Open config.js
5. Replace YOUR_AMAZON_ASSOCIATE_ID with your tracking ID
6. Update product affiliate URLs with your tracking ID
```

### Step 2: Deploy to Netlify (10 minutes)
```
1. Go to: netlify.com
2. Sign up (use Google or GitHub)
3. Drag your folder onto Netlify
4. Done! You get a free URL like: gardenwise.netlify.app
```

### Step 3: Test It Works (5 minutes)
```
1. Visit your new URL
2. Click "Add to Cart" on a product
3. Click "Proceed to Checkout"
4. Verify you're taken to Amazon
```

### Step 4: Create Social Accounts (20 minutes)
```
1. Instagram - @gardenwise (or similar)
2. TikTok - @gardenwise
3. Pinterest - gardenwise
4. Reddit - Create account
5. Follow 50+ gardening accounts
```

### Step 5: Post First Content (10 minutes)
```
1. Take a photo of your garden (or find one online)
2. Write: "Welcome to GardenWise! 🌱 Here for gardening tips, advice, and product recommendations. Follow for weekly tips!"
3. Post on Instagram
4. Share the link in your bio to your Netlify site
```

## 📱 Drive Traffic (Most Important!)

### Pinterest (Best for Affiliate Sales!)
- Create vertical pins (1000x1500px) in Canva
- Use keywords: "beginner gardening", "garden tools", "composting"
- Post 5-10 pins per week
- Link directly to your site
- Takes 2-3 months but converts best

**Example Pin:** "10 Essential Garden Tools for Beginners | Complete Guide" → links to your site

### Instagram Reels (Easy Growth)
- Post 3-4x per week
- 15-30 second videos
- Quick tips, time-lapses, before/afters
- Use trending sounds/music
- Link in bio to your site

**Example:** Time-lapse of seed sprouting with text "From seed to plant in 30 days!"

### TikTok (Viral Potential)
- Post 3-5x per week
- 30-60 second videos
- Hook viewers in first 3 seconds
- Gardening hacks, tips, fails
- Use #GardenTok, #PlantTok, #FYP

**Example:** "5 Gardening Mistakes I Made So You Don't Have To"

### Reddit (Build Authority)
- Post 1-2x per week to r/gardening
- Answer questions genuinely
- Share your guides (don't spam)
- Build karma and credibility
- Mention your site naturally

**Example:** Answer a question thoroughly, then "Full guide on my site: [link]"

### Content Tips
1. **Hook in first 3 seconds** - Critical for TikTok/Reels
