## Key Metrics Explained

**Conversion Rate** = (Sales / Clicks) × 100
- Example: 10 sales / 100 clicks = 10%

**Avg Order Value** = Total Revenue / Number of Sales
- Example: $500 revenue / 10 sales = $50 average

**Bounce Rate** = (Single-page sessions / Total sessions) × 100
- Lower is better (means people explore your site)

**Traffic Sources**
- Direct: People typing your URL
- Organic: Google search
- Social: Instagram, TikTok, Pinterest, Reddit
- Referral: Other websites linking to you
