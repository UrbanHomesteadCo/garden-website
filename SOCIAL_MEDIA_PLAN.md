## Platform-Specific Tips

### Instagram
- Post 3-4x per week
- Reels get 5x more reach than photos
- Best times: Tuesday-Thursday, 6-9 AM
- Use all 30 hashtags (mix popular + niche)
- Save highlights of your best content
- Link in bio to your site

### TikTok
- Post 3-5x per week
- Hook viewers in first 3 seconds
- Use trending sounds/music
- Longer videos (45-60s) get more watch time
- Trending hashtags: #FYP #ForYouPage
- Encourage comments (questions work great)

### Pinterest
- Post 5-10 pins per week
- Best times: 8-11 AM, 7-11 PM
- Use long descriptions (200+ characters)
- Create multiple pins per blog post
- Link directly to your site
- This drives the MOST affiliate sales

### Reddit
- Post 1-2x per week
- Be genuine, not promotional
- Answer questions thoroughly
- Join communities, don't just promote
- Build karma and trust first
- Mention your site naturally

### Email
- Send 1-2x per week
- Tuesday-Thursday best
- Personalize subject lines
- Include 1-2 affiliate products naturally
- High open rates = engaged audience
- Build list consistently

## Engagement Tactics

1. **Respond to EVERY comment** in first hour
2. **Ask questions** in captions
3. **Reply to comments with videos** (TikTok)
4. **Engage with similar accounts** daily
5. **Create shareable content** (tips, hacks)
6. **Use calls-to-action** (visit site, save this, tag a friend)
7. **Go live occasionally** (Q&A, tours, behind-the-scenes)
