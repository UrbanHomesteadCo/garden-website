## Key Success Factors

✅ **Post Consistently**
- Pinterest: 5-10 pins/week
- Instagram: 3-4 posts/week

✅ **Link Back to Website**
- Every pin/post should link to your site
- This drives traffic & affiliate sales

✅ **Engage with Community**
- Respond to comments
- Follow similar accounts
- Share other gardeners' content

✅ **Track What Works**
- Note which pins get clicks
- Note which posts get engagement
- Do more of what works

✅ **Be Patient**
- Growth takes 3-6 months
- Consistency matters more than virality
- Trust the process
